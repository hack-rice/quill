Basic Information

Please note: if you are a Rice Student, you do not need to fill out this form. This form exists to help us better handle applications for students from other schools. Rice students are automatically admitted to HackRice X and do not have to go through an application process. We will send out a Google form at the beginning of the fall semester for you to register.

Another note: Due to the pandemic and Rice University regulations, HackRice X will be remote for all non-Rice participants this year. Please do NOT make travel arrangements. Additionally, please make sure you have access to reliable wifi for the duration of the event.

As a virtual event, we are excited to open up HackRice X to a larger and more diverse pool of hackers! However, we have decided to limit the age of participation to be at least 13 years of age. Checking the box below affirms that you are, or will be, 13 years or older by September 18th, 2020. If you're under the age of 13, and you really want to attend HackRice X, please stop filling out this form and email hack@rice.edu with the title "Under 13 HackRice X Application." We will then begin an alternative application process.
