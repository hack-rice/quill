Because of limitations imposed by Rice, we are not legally allowed to host non-Rice minors (those under 18) for HackRice 9. Checking the box below affirms that you are, or will be, 18 years or older by September 20th, 2019. However, if you're under the age of 18, and you really want to attend HackRice 9, please stop filling out this form and email hack@rice.edu with the title "Minor HackRice 9 Application". We will then begin an alternative application process.

We will be checking ID. If you are a non-Rice minor, you will be turned away at the door.
